Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

Where culinary artistry meets Pacific Northwest heritage

Since 2018, The Maritime Grill has been Seattle's premier destination for Pacific Northwest cuisine. Located in Pike Place Market, we partner with local fishermen and farmers to bring the finest seasonal ingredients to your table.

Led by Executive Chef James Morrison, who brings 20 years of culinary excellence from San Francisco to Vancouver, our kitchen combines classical techniques with innovative Pacific Rim influences to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Our commitment to sustainability has earned us Seattle Magazine's "Best Sustainable Seafood Restaurant" award three years running. From our herb garden to partnerships with responsible fisheries and local farms, we're dedicated to both excellence and environmental stewardship.
